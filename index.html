About Me

I am a 4th year PhD student at Cambridge, supervised by Mihalis Dafermos. I also did my undergraduate and Part III at Cambridge. A full CV is available here.

I am interested in partial differential equations, especially nonlinear wave equations arising in General Relativity. I am currently working on dynamical extensions for shell-crossing singularities in the Einstein-dust system.
